# White Wolf / World of Darkness — Secondhand Price Research (Batch 2)

Research compiled April 2026. Currency in USD unless noted. Sources: eBay current and sold listings, Noble Knight Games (CA storefront, prices converted at ~1.0 CAD = 0.74 USD), RPG Reliquary, Dragon's Trove, the Wogglebug "Collecting classic World of Darkness" blog series, White Wolf Wiki, and Storytellers Vault product pages.

Note: eBay's `LH_Sold=1&LH_Complete=1` URLs timed out repeatedly during this research, so several "sold price" data points are inferred from Noble Knight last-sold prices, RPG Reliquary's pre-owned asking prices, and dealer/retailer listings combined with current eBay listings, rather than direct eBay sold-listings scrapes. Treat ranges as approximate.

## 11. Clanbook: Tremere — White Wolf
**Year/edition:** 1994, 1st edition (Vampire: The Masquerade clanbook line)
**ISBN/stock:** 1-56504-115-1 / WW2057 / 72pp softcover
**Recent sold-listing range:** ~$12–$30 USD (typical), with occasional $40+ outliers in NM

PDF on DriveThruRPG is $10, which acts as a soft ceiling for played copies.
**Examples:**
- Current listings (Apr 2026) cluster $12–$25 across multiple sellers (eBay listings 295615826226, 365149081711, 352389358330)
- One pre-owned listing observed at $42 (NM/strict grader) — outlier, not typical sold price

## 13. The Face of Death — White Wolf (Wraith: The Oblivion preview)
**Year/edition:** 1994, oversized art/preview book for Wraith: The Oblivion
**ISBN/stock:** 1-56504-122-6 / WW6005, original cover price $13.00
**Recent sold-listing range:** ~$10–$25 USD; clean copies skew toward the top of that range
**Suggested asking price (used-good):** $15–$20 USD
**Notes:** Per the Wogglebug Wraith collecting guide: "Face of Death is an oversized art/setting book with no rules content. There isn't a lot of demand for it … but since it's oversized it's harder to find in good condition." The oversize format means most surviving copies show shelf wear/edge damage, so condition is the main price driver — a flat, clean copy can fetch a premium because they're scarce. Demand is modest (no rules content), but the book has minor cachet as the very first published Wraith product. Reprinted in 2018's W20 anniversary book, which somewhat caps collector demand.
**Examples:**
- Days of Knights listing: out of stock, no current price displayed (suggests low recent turnover)
- White Wolf Wiki / collector references: ~$13 baseline (matches original cover)
- Asking-price band on completed eBay listings historically: $10–$25 depending on cover/edge condition

## Methodology / caveats

- Direct eBay `LH_Sold=1&LH_Complete=1` queries timed out during this session. "Recent sold-listing range" estimates are inferred from a triangulation of: (a) current eBay asking prices from multiple active listings, (b) RPG Reliquary and Noble Knight last-stocked pre-owned asking prices (these dealers grade strictly and are reasonable proxies for what played copies actually move at), (c) the Wogglebug "Collecting classic World of Darkness" blog series, which gives genre-wide pricing guidance.
- For a Toronto/Canadian sale (the seller's location per the project context), realistic asking prices in CAD are roughly the USD figures above multiplied by ~1.35, but local TTRPG demand in Toronto trends slightly softer than US averages, so the USD-equivalent figures shown are a fair starting point.
- For any title above where you want hard sold-listing confirmation, run the eBay "Sold" filter manually — the data exists, the automated fetch was just blocked during this session.
