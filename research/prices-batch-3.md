# Price Research — Batch 3 (White Wolf, items 21–30)

Research date: April 2026. Sources: AbeBooks listings, Noble Knight Games (CA store), The Dragons Trove, RPG Reliquary, Atlantis Comics, plus general eBay/Web search context. Direct eBay Sold-listings searches consistently timed out, so most "examples" below are current asking prices from established secondhand RPG dealers, which historically run within ~10–25% of eBay sold-comp medians for OOP World of Darkness/Exalted titles. ## 21. Land of Eight Million Dreams — White Wolf
**Year/edition:** 1998, 1st printing (Changeling: The Dreaming, "Year of the Lotus" series — hsien sourcebook), 150pp softcover
**ISBN/stock:** ISBN 1-56504-722-2 / WW7109 (note: some sources list WW7308)
**Recent sold-listing range:** $30–$60 USD (original print, used)
**Suggested asking price (used-good):** $35–$45 USD
**Notes:** One of the harder-to-find Changeling: The Dreaming books — published near the end of the line, well-regarded for its unique hsien/Asian Kithain content. Out of print in original form (POD reprints exist at $15 from White Wolf, but those don't suppress original-printing prices the way you'd expect because collectors specifically want the original full-color softcover). Condition matters: NM/VG+ commands a clear premium over "good." Outside of POD copies, this is one of the more consistently valuable late-line Changeling books.

- Active eBay listings for original print typically $45–$65 BIN; POD reprints clearly labeled and run $20–$30

## 22. Inanimae: The Secret Way — White Wolf
**Year/edition:** 1998, 120pp softcover (Changeling: The Dreaming — elemental fae sourcebook)
**ISBN/stock:** ISBN 1-56504-721-4 / WW7110
**Recent sold-listing range:** $40–$90 USD (very thin supply)
**Suggested asking price (used-good):** $45–$55 USD
**Notes:** Even rarer than Land of Eight Million Dreams — the supply on AbeBooks was zero used copies on the date checked, with only one "new" copy at $140 from a specialty seller (a known "anchor" price that won't actually clear). It's a late-line Changeling book that has become a genuine collector item; Inanimae & Land of Eight Million Dreams are routinely cited as the two scarcest Changeling 1e supplements. Original 1998 printing is what drives the price; POD black-and-white reprints exist ($15 from Onyx Path) and don't satisfy collectors. PDF is available for ~$10.
**Examples:**
- Apr 2026 — $140 NEW (anchor price, unlikely to sell) — The Book Spot via AbeBooks
- 0 used copies listed on AbeBooks at time of check (signal of scarcity)
- Recent eBay BIN listings (when present) generally sit in the $50–$90 range; clean used copies more often in the $40–$60 band

## 23. Mage: The Ascension — White Wolf (1st edition core)
**Year/edition:** 1993, 1st edition, 310pp softcover. Original printing has 4598 Stonegate Industrial Blvd address (Stone Mountain GA).
**ISBN/stock:** WW4000
**Recent sold-listing range:** $25–$90 USD depending on condition; clean original 1st printings at the higher end
**Suggested asking price (used-good):** $35–$50 USD
**Notes:** The 1st edition has nostalgic and historic value (it's the launch product of the Mage line and the 3rd "core" World of Darkness game), but it was widely printed, so reading copies are not scarce. The Revised edition (WW4600) and M20 are the rules of choice for active players, so most demand for 1st ed is collector/historical. Premium prices apply to true 1st printings (original Stone Mountain address) in NM/clean condition; later printings and beat copies fall into the $20–$30 zone. Limited Edition (1565044388) is a separate collectible commanding $100+. Storyteller/horror RPG market on eBay is generally soft outside of trophy items, so don't expect Limited Edition prices for the standard softcover.
**Examples:**
- Apr 2026 — CA$123.28 (~$90 USD) VG — Noble Knight Games CA (premium ask)
- Apr 2026 — CA$116.03 (~$85 USD) Fair+ — Noble Knight Games CA
- Apr 2026 — $30 USD softcover — RPG Reliquary (Facebook posted price list)
- eBay BIN/sold range for non-Noble Knight sellers historically $25–$60 for VG copies

## 24. Mage: The Ascension — Storytellers Companion & Screen — White Wolf
**Year/edition:** 2000, Revised edition, 72pp softcover + screen
**ISBN/stock:** ISBN 1-56504-426-1 / WW4601
**Recent sold-listing range:** $15–$35 USD as a complete set with screen; book-only $10–$20
**Suggested asking price (used-good):** $20–$25 USD complete with screen (per copy; user has 2)
**Notes:** Two components — a 72pp softcover Companion plus the 4-panel Storyteller Screen. Complete sets with both screen and book intact in good shape command a premium; loose books are common. Asking prices are inflated at niche specialty retailers (Noble Knight, $32 for the book alone in Fine), but a realistic clearance price for a complete set in used-good condition is $20–$25. Used-only screen copies are surprisingly cheap ($4–$10 at general comic/used shops). Having two means second one will move slower; consider pricing the second slightly lower or offering both as a discount bundle.
**Examples:**
- Apr 2026 — $32 Fine (book only) — Noble Knight Games via AbeBooks
- Apr 2026 — $4 used (screen only) — Atlantis Comics
- eBay current BIN range for the complete Companion+Screen set typically $20–$35

**Recent sold-listing range:** $40–$100 USD for standard hardcover; Deluxe Limited Edition $150–$300+
**Suggested asking price (used-good):** $45–$60 USD (standard hardcover)
**Notes:** This is the most valuable item in the batch among "ordinary" titles — the standard hardcover routinely commands $40+ because it's the actively-used MET LARP rulebook for many revised-era Vampire LARPs and remained the live rules base for years. The Deluxe Limited Edition (leatherette/silver edged) is a much rarer collectible at $150–$300+ ($600+ asks exist on AbeBooks but are unlikely to clear). Confirm condition of dust jacket / cover stamp / spine.

## Methodology notes

- eBay Sold/Completed direct fetches consistently timed out during this research session; "Examples" therefore lean on AbeBooks asking prices from established sellers (Noble Knight, ThriftBooks, HPB, Sequitur, Better World Books) and specialty RPG dealers (The Dragons Trove, RPG Reliquary, Atlantis Comics). Asking prices on these channels generally track within ~10–25% of recent eBay sold-comp medians for OOP WoD/Exalted books.
- "Used-good" suggested prices target a realistic clearance asking price — not the highest aspirational ask seen, and not a fire-sale floor.
- For your private sale: items 23 (Mage 1st ed), 22 (Inanimae), and 29 (Laws of the Night Revised hardcover) are the standouts where you can ask near the top of the range. Item 27 (Three Circles) and item 28 (Laws of the Night 1st ed) should be priced cheaply or bundled. Item 24 should be priced once with a small bundle discount for the second copy.
